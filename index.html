How to Use

1. Paste Your Content

Simply paste your LinkedIn post content into the text area. The formatter will automatically count characters, words, and lines.

2. Choose Your Style

Select from various font styles to make your post stand out. Each style is designed to be professional and readable on LinkedIn.

3. Preview and Copy

Preview how your post will look, and when you're satisfied, click the "Copy Text" button to copy it to your clipboard.

Pro Tips:

  • Use different fonts for headlines and body text
  • Keep paragraphs short for better readability
  • Preview in both light and dark modes
